  • How durable is vinyl flooring compared to other flooring options?

    Vinyl flooring is known for its exceptional durability, making it a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces. It is resistant to scratches, stains, and moisture, making it ideal for high-traffic areas like kitchens, bathrooms, and entryways. Additionally, modern advancements in vinyl technology have resulted in enhanced durability, ensuring that your flooring will maintain its beauty and integrity for years to come.

  • Will vinyl flooring installation disrupt my daily routine?

    At ST & Co., we understand the importance of minimizing disruption to your daily routine during the installation process. Our professional installation team works efficiently to complete the project in a timely manner, ensuring that any inconvenience is kept to a minimum. We'll work with you to schedule the installation at a time that is convenient for you, and our team will clean up thoroughly after the job is done, leaving your space looking beautiful and ready to enjoy.

  • Is vinyl flooring easy to maintain?

    One of the key benefits of vinyl flooring is its ease of maintenance. Unlike some other flooring options, vinyl requires minimal upkeep to keep it looking its best. Regular sweeping or vacuuming, along with occasional mopping with a mild detergent, is typically all that's needed to keep vinyl flooring clean and free from debris. Additionally, because vinyl is resistant to stains and moisture, spills can be easily wiped away without causing damage to the flooring surface. Overall, vinyl flooring offers a hassle-free maintenance solution for busy homeowners and businesses alike.

  • Can vinyl flooring be installed over existing flooring?

    Yes, in many cases, vinyl flooring can be installed directly over existing flooring surfaces such as hardwood, laminate, tile, or even concrete, as long as the existing floor is clean, flat, and in good condition. This can save time and money on the installation process, as there's no need for costly and time-consuming floor removal. However, it's essential to ensure proper preparation of the existing floor, including thorough cleaning and leveling if necessary, to ensure a smooth and stable surface for the new vinyl flooring. Our professional installation team at ST & Co is experienced in assessing the suitability of existing flooring for vinyl installation and can provide expert guidance and preparation services to ensure optimal results.
