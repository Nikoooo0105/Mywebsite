  • Can I choose the style and material of the trim for my doors and windows?

    Yes, absolutely! At ST & Co., we understand that every homeowner has unique style preferences and property requirements. That's why we offer a wide range of trim styles and materials to choose from, including wood, composite, PVC, and more. Whether you prefer a classic, traditional look or a modern, contemporary design, our experts will work closely with you to select the perfect trim that complements your home's architecture and reflects your personal taste.

  • Will Door And Window Trim Installation increase energy efficiency in my home?

    Yes, installing trim around your doors and windows can help improve energy efficiency in your home. Properly installed trim creates a tighter seal, reducing air leaks and drafts that can lead to energy loss. Additionally, our team ensures precise measurements and professional installation techniques to maximize the effectiveness of the trim in sealing gaps and improving insulation. As a result, you may experience lower energy bills and increased comfort in your living spaces.

  • How do I maintain and care for the trim after installation?

    Maintaining your newly installed door and window trim is relatively simple. Regular cleaning with a mild soap and water solution can help keep the trim looking its best. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as these can damage the finish. Additionally, inspect the trim periodically for any signs of wear or damage, such as cracks or peeling paint, and address any issues promptly to prevent further damage. With proper care and maintenance, your trim can retain its beauty and functionality for years to come.
